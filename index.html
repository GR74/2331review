Traps to watch for

  • MST vs SPT: Different problems entirely.
  • Dijkstra + negative edges: Breaks, even without cycles.
  • Negative cycle on path: No shortest path exists.
  • K fixed vs K in input: IS of 5 is P; IS of K is NPC.
  • Matching vs VC: Matching is P, VC is NPC.
  • Path compression: Only one path flattens.
  • UBH tie: Second arg under first, first.height += 1.
